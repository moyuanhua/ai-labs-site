AI Chat Assistant is our flagship conversational AI product. It supports multi-turn conversations, file uploads, and integrates seamlessly with your existing tools.

- **Context awareness** — Remembers conversation history
- **File analysis** — Upload PDFs, images, and spreadsheets
- **API access** — Full REST API for programmatic usage
